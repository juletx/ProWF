[Risk Type = Direct or Indirect, Resource, Business, Technical, Schedule
Impact is a numerical value, usually between 1 and 5, that represents the impact to the project if the risk comes true.
5 is a HIGH IMPACT and 1 is LOW IMPACT.
Probability is the percentage chance that a risk will occur.
Magnitude is the result of the multiplication of Probability and Impact, providing a joint metric suitable for sorting.]
